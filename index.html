Right now I'm training as a SOC analyst at UT's Information Security Office, working on hardware and security research, building embedded systems with an AI & robotics team, and software engineering at LHD.

I love philosophy, discussion, and teaching, especially when it lines up with the technical topics I nerd out about. I'm working on a few papers, technical writeups, and essays about the projects I'm building that you should check out!
